Common Questions

Got
Questions?

How long does a typical roof installation take?
add

Most residential projects are completed in 1–3 days depending on square footage and material complexity. Commercial projects range from one week to several months. We provide a detailed project timeline during your free inspection, and our project management team provides daily progress updates.

What does your 50-year warranty cover?
add

Our Elite tier 50-year structural warranty covers material defects, installation workmanship, and structural failure under normal weather conditions. It includes annual inspection visits and priority repair response at no additional cost. The warranty is transferable to new property owners.

Do you offer financing for roofing projects?
add

Yes. We offer flexible financing plans through our banking partners, including 0% APR for 18 months on qualifying projects, and extended payment plans up to 10 years. We also assist with insurance claims documentation at no additional charge.

What roofing materials do you specialize in?
add

We work with the full spectrum: architectural asphalt shingles, standing seam and corrugated metal, natural and synthetic slate, clay and concrete tile, TPO and EPDM for flat roofs, modified bitumen, built-up roofing, and living green roof systems. Materials are specified based on your climate data, not preference.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
add

Our emergency response teams are on standby 24/7/365. We guarantee on-site arrival within 2 hours for all emergency calls in our service area. We begin with immediate weatherproofing to prevent further damage, then schedule permanent repair at your earliest convenience.

Do you handle insurance claims?
add

Absolutely. Our dedicated claims team works directly with all major insurers. We document all storm damage with our drone technology, provide detailed damage reports, and can attend the adjuster inspection on your behalf. Most storm damage repairs are covered 100% by homeowners insurance.

Still Have Questions?

Our senior roofing consultants are available Monday through Saturday, 7am to 7pm, to answer any questions about your specific project — no obligation, no pressure.
